Refund Policy

- HOW DO I KNOW I AM ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND?

You are eligible for a refund in the following cases:

  • You have bought the SIM from @ VIPNumber.com.
  • If You Don’t Receive UPC (Unique Porting Code) within 24 working Hours of Purchase.
  • If Your Order is Cancelled By Us for Any Reason.
  • Occurring in our systems or issues after booking process.

- HOW MUCH OF THE AMOUNT PAID IS REFUNDED AFTER ORDER CANCELATION?

We have a 100% refund policy. Whatever you have paid for your VIP Number will be refunded if the order is cancelled.

If you cancel your order after making a purchase, we will process a refund to your original payment method. However, we will deduct the payment gateway fees from the refund amount to cover the costs incurred by the payment processor.

- WHAT WILL HAPPEN IF THE PORTING REQUEST IS REJECTED BY THE OPERATOR?

We will refund 100% amount in case your number porting request has been rejected by the operator, for any reason.

- HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO PROCESS A REFUND?

Please allow 4-7 working days for your Refund to be processed. In case UPC Shared it may take 20-25 working Days.

- HOW CAN I PLACE A REFUND REQUEST?

To place a refund request, you can share your details on email at support@vipnumber.com or call at +91 9222 222 007.

If approved, the refund will be processed and the amount will be credited to your original mode of payment within 4 to 7 working days.

- WHAT DO I DO IF MY QUESTION ISN’T COVERED HERE?

If you cannot find the answer to your question here, you can contact our customer services team on sales@vipnumber.com or give a call at +91 9222 222 007.

If you have already placed your Refund Request, please give us Order details for better understanding. Our customer service team is available Monday to Saturday from 10am to 7pm to assist you.

Please note: We do not accept any refunds post activation of the number.
